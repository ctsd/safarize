Safarize, photo challenge game

Have fun with your friends, sending each other photographic challenges. Who will be the best ? You can also play on public challenges for more awesomeness and competition. The world is at your feet, take it in photo ! Don't hesitate ! Join us !

Challenge your friends

Send challenges to your friends ! They can be simple: "Snap your cat" or really difficult: "Take a selfie on the top of the Everest". You can do everything in Safarize.

Win points

Each photo and challenge brings you points or badges. Get a maximum of them to become a photograph master !

Limitless

Your playground is the entire world. Too large and evolutive to have to time to get bored ! Many challenges are also available.
